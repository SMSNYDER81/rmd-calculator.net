Required Minimum Distributions,
demystified.

A free tool built to cut through the complexity of RMD rules across every era of tax law.

RMD Calculator is a free tool that helps retirees and inherited IRA beneficiaries calculate their Required Minimum Distributions — without needing to hire an accountant just to figure out the basic number.

The rules around RMDs have changed significantly over the past several years. The SECURE Act of 2019, the SECURE 2.0 Act of 2022, and a series of IRS proposed and final regulations have created a landscape where the right calculation depends heavily on when you inherited the account, how old you are, and what kind of account it is. Most online calculators ignore this entirely and give a single, often wrong, answer.

This calculator covers all major rule eras — original owner IRA and 401(k) accounts, pre-SECURE Act inherited accounts using the stretch IRA method, SECURE 1.0 accounts under the 10-year rule, and SECURE 2.0 accounts under the final IRS regulations. You get the right rules for your situation, not a one-size-fits-all estimate.

The tool also provides a multi-year projection so you can see how your RMDs will change over time as your account balance and life expectancy factors shift — useful for tax planning and understanding the full picture, not just this year's number.

Everything runs in plain English. Each result includes an explanation of which rules apply to your situation and why, so you actually understand what you're looking at rather than just getting a number with no context.
